Yuraive is a media player for branching experiences made from audio and images.

Add libraries from folders on your device, SMB shares, or WebDAV servers. Yuraive supports audio, video, images, WebVTT subtitles, background playback, history, favorites, shuffle, and restoring the previous playback state.

Yuraive works can define scene transitions triggered by playback completion or buttons, per-work playback controls, custom layouts, and Starlark scripts.

Connection with Yuraive for Windows uses a QR code and transfers media directly between devices. No account registration or advertising is required.
